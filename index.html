About us

zongda lighting

Zongda Lighting Company is a Tuniso-Chinese company created in 2019 and specializing in the field of urban electronic display and automatic solution.

Our mission is to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments through the power of light. We believe that lighting is not just a functional element but a design feature that can enhance aesthetics, create ambiance, and improve overall well-being

With this belief at our core, we strive to deliver lighting solutions that go beyond illumination, captivating and engaging the senses.
